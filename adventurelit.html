Adventure Literature

One of Mr Brown's favorite classes to teach is his Adventure Literature class open to Seniors. In this class students dive into stories of risk, discovery, self exploration, and the human desire to push the boundries of what is familiar. This class looks at the many motivations behind adventure. Alongside reading several powerful texts about this topic students will also write about their own experiences with adventure. The class is concluded with a hike to Mount Monadnock or Mount Wachusett!
