Favorite Hiking Conditions

Season: Fall 100%.
Intensity (Easy, Moderate, Hard): He usually choose the hardest, most strenuous hike.
Time of Day to Hike: Sunrise. He likes to be the first person on the trail that day.
Weather: Blue skies :)
Temperature Range: 65 degrees with a slight breeze is my idea of heaven.
Scenery Type: Exposed mountains so the views are breathtaking.
Group Size: Ridin' solo, ridin' solo.
Ideal Length of Hike: Longer the better. The longest day hike he's done was over 18 miles (Owl's Head in NH).
Elevation: Give him the steepest, most exposed trail you can find, like Huntington Ravine Trail or the North Slide of Tripyramid.
Snacks or Trail Food: He always carry Gummi Bears or Sour Patch Kids!
