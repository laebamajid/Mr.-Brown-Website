Favorite Hikes

Franconia Ridge

The Franconia Range is a mountain range located in the White Mountains in the state of New Hampshire. It is the second highest range of peals in the White Mountains. This is an 8.6 mile loop near Lincoln, New Hampshire. This is considered a challenging route. It is a very popular area for hiking and snowshoeing. This ridge includes Little Haystack Mountain, Mount Lincoln, and Mount Lafayette.

Angel's Landing

Angel's Landing is a 1,488 foot tall rock formation in Zion National Park located in Utah. This is a 4.3 mile hike. It is generally considered a highly challenging route, and should be attempted by experienced hikers. This is a very popular area for hiking! The best times to visit this trail is during March through November.The Angel Landing Trail is a technical route in the Zion National Park and has steep drop-offs and very narrow sections.

The Mist Trail

The Mist trail is one of the most popular hikes in Yosemite National Park located in California. There is an excellent view from Vernal fall from the footbridge. The hike is a relatively short hike, but is still one of the favorites!

Laugavegur Trail

The Laugavegur Trail is a multi-day hiking trail in Iceland, which spans over approximately 55 kilometers, or 34 miles. It is known for its diverse and scenic landscapes with hot springs, black lava fields, and glaciers.
